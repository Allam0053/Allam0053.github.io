### PLASMAHERO

**Volunteer, IT Team**

Jul 2021 - Jan 2022

- Maintained the website of plasmahero.help, which is used to handle registration of convalescent plasma donors and requesting convalescent plasma
- Improved website functionality and reliability through ongoing maintenance and updates
- Organized a webinar to socialize about self-isolation during the COVID-19 pandemic with over 300 participants
- Improved communication and outreach skills through the organization and facilitation of the webinar
- Contributed to the COVID-19 response efforts by providing information on self-isolation and facilitating the registration and request of convalescent plasma through the website.
