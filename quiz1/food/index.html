FOOD

CENIL

Cenil is a snack made from starch and then topped with brown sugar. This snack is very suitable to be eaten in any circumstances. The sweet taste of brown sugar is very appetizing. But cenil sellers usually only sell in the morning. There are rarely cenil depots that are open day to night.

ES OYEN

Es oyen is a drink containing sliced fruit. Usually the fruit is avocado, jackfruit, and mango. In addition, jelly can be added. This drink is perfect for drinking during the day

PECEL

Blitar also has culinary foods such as pecel. But pecel is very common in the residency of Kediri. Pecel contains vegetables such as spinach and bean sprouts then topped with peanut sauce and served with dents (baca: peyek). Pecel is very suitable as a daily food.
